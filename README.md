## 3. Transition Turns (Preparing to Move)
These commands turn the squad specifically to form a column for marching.

| Command (Formal Malay) | Meaning / Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| Bergerak Ke Kanan, Bertiga-tiga... <br>Ke Kanan... Pusing | Move to the Right. (Forms a column of 3 moving Right) |
| Bergerak Ke Kiri, Bertiga-tiga... <br>Ke Kiri... Pusing | Move to the Left. (Forms a column of 3 moving Left) |
| Akan Menghadap Ke Hadapan... <br>Ke Belakang... Pusing | Intent: Face the Front.<br>Action: About Turn.<br>(Used when the squad is currently facing the Rear) |
| Akan Menghadap Ke Belakang... <br>Ke Belakang... Pusing | Intent: Face the Rear.<br>Action: About Turn.<br>(Used when the squad is currently facing the Front) |

## 6. Sizing Command (Paras)

Used to arrange the squad by height (Tallest on the Right, Shortest on the Left).

**Step 1: The Order**

"Yang tinggi ke kanan, rendah ke kiri... dalam satu barisan... Paras!"
(Tall ones to the right, short ones to the left... in one rank... Size!)

**Step 2: Numbering**

"Dari kanan... Bilang!"
(From the right... Number!)
Troops shout: "Satu, Dua, Satu, Dua..."

**Step 3: Breaking Ranks**

"Nombor ganjil satu langkah ke hadapan, nombor genap satu langkah ke belakang... Gerak!"
(Odd numbers one step forward, even numbers one step back... Move!)

**Step 4: Turning**

"Orang yang di sebelah kanan diam... Nombor ganjil ke kanan, nombor genap ke kiri... Barisan kanan dan kiri... Pusing!"
(Right marker stay put... Odd numbers turn right, even numbers turn left... ranks right and left... Turn!)

**Step 5: Forming Squad**

"Jadikan... Skuad!"
(Form... Squad!)
Troops march into their new positions to form a standard 3-row block.
